The Rainy Days story

Rainy Days was created in 1994 by siblings Ann and Peter Larsen in Oslo. They grew up with adventureous parents who loved exploring nature in and around Oslo, but also in other parts of Norway. Ann grew up as an avid skiier and compete nationally through her teen years. Peter, on the other hand, love the extreme and completed his first sky dive at only 19 years old. Together, the sibling pair wanted to create a brand focused on sustainability, durability, and ethical resources. Therefore, all Rainy Days jackets are made of recycleable materials in Spain, at factories where the workers recieve fair wages. Our jackets all come with a 5 year warranty, and we mend your jacket free of charge if anything happens to your garment before the warranty expires. Rainy Days consists of jackets made for all purposes, be it a light hike in the woods, long camping trips in the middle of a rainstorm, or a ski trip in sub-zero conditions. No matter where your next adventure takes you, Rainy Days have a jacket suitable for your exploration, be it by foot, skiis, or kayak.
